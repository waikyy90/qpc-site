Interactome analysis (IP-MS / ChIP-MS / proximity)​

ChIP-MS reveals the local chromatin composition by label-free quantitative proteomics

Yong et al., bioRxiv (2023)

ChIP-MS interrogation of chromatin-associated protein complexes using genetic knockout controls.

Research summary
Chromatin immunoprecipitation coupled to label-free quantitative mass spectrometry (ChIP-MS) was used to characterise proteins associated with ZBTB48 at telomeric regions. A WT vs knockout comparison enabled discrimination of specific versus background binding.

QPC contribution
Experimental design, ChIP-MS workflow optimisation, quantitative proteomics analysis, and data interpretation.

  • DOI: https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2023.01.27.525999v1
