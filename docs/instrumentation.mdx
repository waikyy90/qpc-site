## Liquid chromatography–mass spectrometry (LC–MS)

High-resolution LC–MS systems are operated for quantitative proteomics and interactome studies, supporting both discovery-oriented and targeted applications.

**Platforms include:**
- Bruker timsTOF fleX mass spectrometer  
- Thermo Fisher Scientific Q Exactive HF mass spectrometer  
- Evosep One liquid chromatography system  

---

## Imaging mass spectrometry

Imaging mass spectrometry platforms support spatially resolved proteomics and tissue-based studies.

**Platforms include:**
- Bruker timsTOF fleX MALDI imaging mass spectrometry capabilities  

---

## Sample processing

Front-end sample processing instrumentation supports reproducible preparation of biological samples prior to mass spectrometry analysis.

**Platforms include:**
- Bruker BeatBox tissue homogenizer
