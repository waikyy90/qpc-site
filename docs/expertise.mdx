# Expertise
<br />

Our expertise spans quantitative proteomics approaches for studying protein abundance, interactions, and spatial organization. We support a broad range of biological questions across discovery, hypothesis-driven, and translational studies.

**Study types and applications**

Our expertise supports a range of study types, including:
- Discovery and comparative proteomics
- Mechanistic studies and pathway analysis
- Clinical and translational research
- Tissue-based and spatial proteomics applications
