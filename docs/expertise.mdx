# Expertise
<br />

This page outlines the core proteomics capabilities available through the Quantitative Proteomics Core.

## What we offer

We provide quantitative proteomics expertise across multiple experimental scales and biological contexts, supporting both discovery-driven and hypothesis-driven research.

- Comparative and perturbation-based proteomics studies  
- Mechanistic interrogation of protein interactions and molecular complexes  
- Spatially resolved proteomics in tissue contexts  
- Translational and clinically relevant sample analysis
